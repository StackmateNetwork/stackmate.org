STACKMATE

Tools for financial independence.

Combining a mobile bitcoin wallet with e2ee communications.


Multiple Descriptor Wallets

Powered by bdk aka Magical Bitcoin; stackmate's core is a powerful descriptor wallet that can extend to support a wide range of bitcoin scripts.

Easily manage, recover and move between multiple wallets; including contracts with others.


Connect Your Node

Stay sovereign, maintain privacy and avoid possible censorship by connecting to your own node (electrum).


Manage Cold Storage

Use your cold storage xpub to watch and manage your cold storage accounts.

Pairs perfectly with the ColdCard.


Communicate Privately

Join/Self-host a private e2ee server and build your network. Coordinate multi-sig contracts for various use-cases including vaults, escrow trades or loans.
