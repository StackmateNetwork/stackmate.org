STACKMATE

A mobile bitcoin key manager.

Enabling descriptor wallets and private communications.


USE DESCRIPTOR WALLETS

Powered by bdk aka Magical Bitcoin; stackmate's core is a powerful descriptor wallet that can extend to support a wide range of bitcoin scripts.

Easily manage, recover (segwit/taproot only) and move between multiple wallets.

CONNECT YOUR NODE

Stay sovereign, maintain privacy and avoid possible censorship by connecting to your own node (electrum).

MANAGE COLD STORAGE

Use your cold storage xpub to watch and manage your cold storage accounts.

Pairs perfectly with the ColdCard.

COMMUNICATE PRIVATELY

Join/Self-host a private e2ee server and build your network (cypherpost). Coordinate multi-sig contracts for various use-cases including vaults, escrow trades or loans.

All comms are Torified by default.

Incognito Keyboard is enforced.


SECURITY NOTICE:

DO NOT USE WITHOUT READING!

Stackmate is in early beta. It is safe to use, as long as you are aware of the following:

Always write down (BACKUP) your seed!

BACKUP LATER FEATURE IS UNSAFE AND MEANT FOR TESTERS. BACKUP ASAP!

Updates currently erase old state and require you to reimport your seed. This will be fixed in the next major release. Disable auto-updates to be safe.
